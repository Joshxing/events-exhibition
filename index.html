About Our Event

13-14 November 2024

Calling all campus and security leaders! Come join us in Las Vegas this upcoming November 13-14th, 2024 for School Safety and Security Conference. This unique event brings together a passionate group of senior campus and security leaders from all over the United States for two days of discussions about the biggest challenges the students and schools are facing today. Highlighting topics like Protecting Kids from Gun Violence, Bomb threats, School violence, Dropout, attendees can strategize, collaborate, network, and discuss solutions to the security threats that keep us up at night. Bring your passion, your ideas, and your dedication to be part of the movement that helps protect all our kids. Apply to speak at this year’s conference by 30th April 2024 – and don’t forget to save the date for November 13-14! Questions? Reach out to info@schoolresponseconfrence.org. We can't wait to see you in Las Vegas!
