About Our Event

27 - 28 June 2023

Welcome to the School Response Conference in Jacksonville, Florida! We are excited to bring together the safety and security community to explore the latest technology and market trends, share best practices, and collaborate on the biggest challenges facing students and schools. From gun violence, to bomb threats, to gang activity and dating violence, no one can ignore the pressing issues that plague students and school safety officers. But we believe that together we can create a better future for our young people. Gain valuable insights from industry leaders and peers, find actionable solutions, and improved ways of preventing threats and responding to emergencies. We invite you to join us in Jacksonville on June 27-28, 2023, and be a part of this essential conference. Don’t miss out – applications for all speakers close on March 30th so make sure you submit your application on time. For more information please email us at info@schoolresponseconfrence.org. See you there!
